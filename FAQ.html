Embarkation takes place at the Durban Cruise Terminal (KCT – KwaZulu Natal Cruise Terminal). Your check-in time will be clearly stated on your cruise ticket — please arrive only during your allocated time to avoid delays.
To board, you’ll need:
A valid passport (with at least 6 months’ validity), Your printed cruise ticket
Disembarkation happens on the final morning upon return to Durban, typically between 06h00 and 09h00 on Monday. To ensure a smooth process, guests are assigned disembarkation groups, and instructions will be placed in your cabin the night before arrival.
Before exiting the terminal, all guests must:

  • Clear customs, Present their passport to South African immigration authorities
