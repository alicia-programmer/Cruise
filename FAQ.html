FAQs


What’s included in the cruise package?

Accommodation, meals, entertainment, port taxes, and access to onboard facilities.

Do I need a passport?

Yes, a valid passport is required for cross border & international travel.

Is transport to Durban included?

Not included in the price but available upon request for an additional amount.

Can I choose my cabin?

Yes, based on availability and budget.

Are themed parties mandatory?

No, but they're highly recommended for fun!

Is there a payment plan?

Yes, flexible payment options are available.

Any age restrictions?

Guests must be above 18 years of age to attend.

Can I book with friends and keep cabins close?

Yes, request adjacent cabins during booking; subject to availability.

What are the themes on the cruise?

Netflix & Chill (PJs), Hawaiian Party, Poolside Paradise, Masquerade Suits & Sneakers, Hollywood Glamour, Come As Your Type (disco).

What payment options are available onboard?

Cashless system with a cruise card; settle at end by cash or card.

Is Wi‑Fi available onboard?

Yes, Wi‑Fi packages are available for purchase. Signal may vary at sea.

How does Embarkation & Disembarkation work?

Embark at Durban Cruise Terminal (KCT) at your stated time with passport and printed ticket. Disembark on final morning; follow assigned groups and immigration/customs instructions.

What if I need to cancel?

Cancellations follow our policy. You may find a replacement (25% replacement fee applies). Deposits are non‑refundable but transferable with a fee.

What if I want to travel alone?

Share with a matched traveller (same gender) or pay a single supplement for your own cabin.

Group of three?

3/4 sleeper cabins may be available. Contact us for options and pricing.

Non‑South African citizens?

Welcome! Ensure you meet your country’s travel requirements and be in South Africa for departure.
